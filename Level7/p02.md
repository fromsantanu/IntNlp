In the previous chapter, we learned about **probability**.

**How do we train NLP models to perform well?**

👉 The answer lies in:

# 🔹 Optimization Techniques

---

## 🔹 What is Optimization?

👉 Optimization means:

**Adjusting model parameters to reduce errors and improve performance**

---

### 🔹 Simple Idea

* Model makes prediction
* Compare with correct answer
* Adjust model to reduce mistake

---

## 🔹 Real-Life Analogy

Think of learning to throw a ball into a basket:

* First try → miss ❌
* Adjust angle → try again
* Improve over time

👉 That adjustment process = optimization

---

## 🔹 Key Components of Optimization

---

## 🔸 1. Loss Function

👉 Measures how wrong the model is

---

### Example:

* Prediction = “Positive”
* Actual = “Negative”

👉 High loss

---

### Common Loss Functions:

* Cross-Entropy Loss (for classification)
* Mean Squared Error (for regression)

---

## 🔸 2. Gradient Descent

👉 Main algorithm for optimization

---

## 🔹 Idea

👉 Move parameters in direction that reduces loss

---

### 🔹 Intuition

* Imagine standing on a hill
* You want to reach the lowest point

---

## 🔸 Types of Gradient Descent

---

### 🔸 1. Batch Gradient Descent

* Uses entire dataset
* Accurate but slow

---

### 🔸 2. Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD)

* Uses one example at a time
* Faster but noisy

---

### 🔸 3. Mini-Batch Gradient Descent

* Uses small batches
* Balanced approach

---

## 🔸 3. Learning Rate

👉 Step size in optimization

---

### Example:

* Large step → may overshoot ❌
* Small step → slow learning ❌

👉 Need a balanced value

---

## 🔸 4. Advanced Optimizers

---

### 🔹 1. Momentum

👉 Uses past gradients to smooth updates

---

### 🔹 2. RMSProp

👉 Adjusts learning rate dynamically

---

### 🔹 3. Adam (Most Popular)

👉 Combines:

* Momentum
* Adaptive learning rate

---

## 🔹 Why Adam is Popular?

* Fast
* Stable
* Works well in NLP

---

## 🔸 5. Overfitting & Regularization

---

## 🔹 Overfitting

👉 Model learns training data too well

👉 Fails on new data

---

### Example:

* Memorizes answers instead of understanding

---

## 🔹 Regularization Techniques

---

### 🔸 1. Dropout

👉 Randomly remove neurons during training

---

### 🔸 2. L2 Regularization

👉 Penalize large weights

---

## 🔸 6. Backpropagation

---

## 🔹 What is Backpropagation?

👉 Method to update weights using gradients

---

## 🔹 Simple Idea

1. Forward pass → compute output
2. Calculate loss
3. Backward pass → compute gradients
4. Update weights

---

## 🔹 Why Important?

👉 Core mechanism behind training neural networks

---

## 🔹 Optimization in NLP Models

Used in:

* Word embeddings (Word2Vec)
* RNN, LSTM, GRU
* Transformers
* LLMs

---

## 🔹 Challenges in Optimization

* Vanishing gradients
* Slow convergence
* Local minima
* Large computation cost

---

## 🔹 Real-Life Analogy (Revisited)

Think of improving your exam score:

* Study → test → improve
* Repeat until better

👉 That loop = optimization

---

## 🔹 Summary

* Optimization improves model performance
* Key concepts:

  * Loss function
  * Gradient descent
  * Learning rate
  * Optimizers (Adam)
* Prevent overfitting using regularization
* Backpropagation is core training method
