# 🔹 Healthcare NLP & Clinical Text Analysis

---

## 🔹 What is Healthcare NLP?

👉 Healthcare NLP focuses on:

**Understanding and analyzing medical text using NLP techniques**

---

### 🔹 Sources of Medical Text

* Doctor’s notes
* Electronic Health Records (EHR)
* Lab reports
* Prescriptions
* Clinical research papers

---

## 🔹 Real-Life Example

Doctor writes:

> “Patient shows mild fever, BP high, prescribed paracetamol”

👉 NLP system extracts:

* Symptom → fever
* Condition → high BP
* Medicine → paracetamol

---

## 🔹 Why Healthcare NLP is Important

* Improve diagnosis
* Reduce manual work
* Assist doctors
* Analyze large medical data

---

## 🔹 Real-Life Analogy

Think of a medical assistant:

* Reads patient reports
* Highlights important details
* Helps doctor make decisions

👉 Healthcare NLP acts like this assistant

---

# 🔸 1. Clinical Text Characteristics

---

## 🔹 Clinical Text is Different

Healthcare text is:

* Short and incomplete
* Full of abbreviations
* Highly technical

---

### Example:

> “Pt c/o SOB, BP ↑, Rx given”

👉 Meaning:

* Pt → Patient
* SOB → Shortness of breath
* BP ↑ → High blood pressure
* Rx → Treatment

---

👉 This makes NLP more challenging

# 🔸 2. Key Tasks in Healthcare NLP

---

## 🔹 1. Named Entity Recognition (NER)

👉 Identify medical entities:

* Diseases
* Symptoms
* Drugs
* Procedures

---

### Example:

> “Patient has diabetes and hypertension”

👉 Output:

* Diabetes → Disease
* Hypertension → Disease

---

## 🔹 2. Clinical Coding

👉 Map text to standard codes

---

### Example:

* Diabetes → ICD code

---

## 🔹 3. Relation Extraction

👉 Identify relationships

---

### Example:

* Drug → treats → disease

---

## 🔹 4. Text Classification

👉 Classify reports

---

### Example:

* Normal / abnormal
* Disease type

---

## 🔹 5. Clinical Summarization

👉 Convert long reports into short summaries

---
