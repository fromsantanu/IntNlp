# 📘 Chapter 4.5 — Modern NLP Tasks (Generation, QA, Summarization)

---

So far, we have learned:

* Transformers and pretrained models
* Attention and embeddings
* Fine-tuning and prompting

👉 Now we explore how all these are used in **real-world advanced tasks**

---

# 🔹 What are Modern NLP Tasks?

👉 These are tasks where models can:

* Generate human-like text
* Answer questions intelligently
* Summarize long content

👉 These tasks require **deep understanding + generation ability**

---

## 🔹 Overview of Tasks

We will cover:

1. Text Generation
2. Question Answering (QA)
3. Text Summarization

---

# 🔸 1. Text Generation

---

## 🔹 What is Text Generation?

👉 The model creates **new text** based on input

---

## 🔹 Example

Input:

> “Once upon a time…”

👉 Output:

> “there lived a king in a distant land…”

---

## 🔹 How it Works

* Predict next word step-by-step
* Uses probability + context

---

## 🔹 Types of Text Generation

---

### 🔸 1. Open-ended Generation

* Stories
* Articles
* Conversations

---

### 🔸 2. Controlled Generation

* Generate text with rules
* Example: “Write a formal email”

---

# 🔸 2. Question Answering (QA)

---

## 🔹 What is QA?

👉 The model answers questions based on given text or knowledge

---

## 🔹 Example

Context:

> “The capital of India is New Delhi”

Question:

> “What is the capital of India?”

👉 Answer:

> “New Delhi”

---

## 🔹 Types of QA

---

### 🔸 1. Extractive QA

👉 Answer is taken directly from text

---

### 🔸 2. Abstractive QA

👉 Model generates answer in its own words

---

# 🔸 3. Text Summarization

---

## 🔹 What is Summarization?

👉 Convert long text into a **short summary**

---

## 🔹 Example

Input:

> Long news article

👉 Output:

> Short summary (2–3 lines)

---

## 🔹 Types

---

### 🔸 1. Extractive Summarization

👉 Select important sentences

---

### 🔸 2. Abstractive Summarization

👉 Generate new sentences

---

# 🔹 How These Tasks Use Transformers

All modern tasks use:

* Attention mechanism
* Large pretrained models
* Fine-tuning or prompting

---

## 🔹 Example Models

* GPT → Text generation
* BERT → QA
* T5 → All tasks

---

## 🔹 Real-Life Analogy

Think of a smart assistant:

* Text Generation → writes content
* QA → answers your questions
* Summarization → gives short notes

👉 One system doing multiple intelligent tasks

---

## 🔹 Comparison

| Task          | Input              | Output        |
| ------------- | ------------------ | ------------- |
| Generation    | Prompt             | New text      |
| QA            | Question + context | Answer        |
| Summarization | Long text          | Short summary |

---

## 🔹 Limitations

* May produce incorrect answers
* Needs large data
* Can be biased

---

## 🔹 Summary

* Modern NLP tasks combine **understanding + generation**
* Key tasks:

  * Text Generation
  * Question Answering
  * Summarization
* Powered by Transformers and pretrained models
* Widely used in real-world applications
