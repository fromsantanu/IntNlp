# 📘 Chapter 5.5 — Domain-Specific NLP (Healthcare, Legal, Finance)

---

So far, we have learned general NLP techniques.

👉 But in real life, language is **different in different domains**.

For example:

* Medical language is different from legal language
* Financial reports use different terms

👉 This leads to:

# 🔹 Domain-Specific NLP

---

## 🔹 What is Domain-Specific NLP?

👉 Applying NLP techniques to **specific industries or fields**

---

### 🔹 Simple Idea

* General NLP → understands everyday language
* Domain NLP → understands **specialized language**

---

## 🔹 Real-Life Analogy

Think of doctors, lawyers, and bankers:

* All use language
* But each uses **special vocabulary**

👉 NLP models must learn these differences

---

## 🔹 Why Domain-Specific NLP is Needed

General models may fail in specialized areas:

### Example:

> “The patient shows positive symptoms”

👉 In general NLP → “positive” = good
👉 In healthcare → “positive” = disease detected ❗

---

👉 So domain knowledge is very important

---

# 🔹 Key Differences from General NLP

| Feature    | General NLP      | Domain NLP               |
| ---------- | ---------------- | ------------------------ |
| Vocabulary | Common words     | Specialized terms        |
| Data       | General datasets | Domain-specific datasets |
| Accuracy   | Moderate         | Must be high             |
| Complexity | Medium           | High                     |

---

## 🔹 How Domain NLP is Built

---

### Step 1: Collect Domain Data

* Medical records
* Legal documents
* Financial reports

---

### Step 2: Preprocess Text

* Handle domain-specific terms

---

### Step 3: Use Specialized Models

* BioBERT, LegalBERT, FinBERT

---

### Step 4: Fine-tune Model

* Train on domain-specific tasks

---

## 🔹 Real-Life Analogy (Revisited)

Think of specialization:

* General doctor → basic knowledge
* Specialist doctor → deep expertise

👉 Domain NLP = specialist model

---

## 🔹 Challenges in Domain NLP

* Limited labeled data
* Privacy and security issues
* High cost of annotation
* Need for expert knowledge

---

## 🔹 Modern Trends

* Domain-adaptive pretraining
* Few-shot learning
* Multimodal systems (text + images in healthcare)

---

## 🔹 Summary

* Domain-specific NLP focuses on **specialized fields**
* Key domains:

  * Healthcare
  * Legal
  * Finance
* Requires:

  * Specialized data
  * Fine-tuned models
* More accurate but more complex than general NLP
