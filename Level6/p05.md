# 📘 Chapter 6.5 — Efficient NLP (Compression, Quantization)

---

So far, we have learned about **Large Language Models (LLMs)**.

👉 But there is a big problem:

* These models are **very large**
* Require **high memory and computation**
* Difficult to run on normal devices

👉 This leads to an important topic:

# 🔹 Efficient NLP

---

## 🔹 What is Efficient NLP?

👉 Efficient NLP focuses on:

* Making models **smaller**
* Making them **faster**
* Reducing **memory usage**

👉 Without losing too much accuracy

---

## 🔹 Why Efficiency is Important

---

### Example:

* Large model → runs on powerful servers
* Efficient model → runs on mobile phones

---

👉 Applications:

* Mobile apps
* Edge devices
* Real-time systems

---

## 🔹 Real-Life Analogy

Think of a big book:

* Full book → detailed but heavy
* Summary → smaller, faster to read

👉 Efficient NLP = smart summary of models

---

## 🔹 Main Techniques

We will cover:

1. Model Compression
2. Quantization
3. Distillation (bonus concept)

---

# 🔸 1. Model Compression

---

## 🔹 What is Compression?

👉 Reduce model size by removing unnecessary parts

---

## 🔹 Techniques

---

### 🔸 1. Pruning

👉 Remove less important connections

---

### Example:

* Neural network has many connections
* Remove weak ones

---

### 🔸 2. Weight Sharing

👉 Use same weights for similar parts

---

### 🔸 3. Low-Rank Factorization

👉 Break large matrices into smaller ones

---

## 🔹 Advantage

* Smaller model size
* Faster inference

---

# 🔸 2. Quantization

---

## 🔹 What is Quantization?

👉 Reduce precision of numbers

---

### Example:

* Original → 32-bit numbers
* Quantized → 8-bit numbers

---

👉 Less memory required

---

## 🔹 Visualization

![Image](https://images.openai.com/static-rsc-4/SVm-RybuxXF-tHbNxFytCj2GnJW6flFuu5IKhrJmpJGZpAMisAvd7Xu5c77htWIA9FYXJe_FTyMrORzPpg0NRrxH4SkmZyk_yFVE7gQy0NKSiRqIXKaT6iCgUp7neT4oA5IWfqnYjVEcLib50t3ZGgz-OKyUnS6aClcQMO8xkj3UCUps6Sw9tqAPZ-0s8fUv?purpose=fullsize)

---

## 🔹 Types of Quantization

---

### 🔸 1. Post-Training Quantization

👉 Apply after training

---

### 🔸 2. Quantization-Aware Training

👉 Train model with quantization in mind

---

## 🔹 Advantage

* Faster computation
* Lower memory usage

---

## 🔹 Trade-off

* Slight drop in accuracy

---

# 🔸 3. Knowledge Distillation

---

## 🔹 What is Distillation?

👉 Train a small model using a large model

---

### Example:

* Large model → teacher
* Small model → student

👉 Student learns from teacher

---

## 🔹 Visualization

![Image](https://images.openai.com/static-rsc-4/kn8cNhircCFRQ2_GD91iaTmqas_BKnkHeyI-NlgaXhrlgcFXuHEtCCa8LCFQsWhN8Q5wnDUpsaX0LSQCWINgvMib60tSg6gEx4YSL5eutOl0ds2H6qsEbwxEJBTX044PkVlp6rcDlSYadtgHuSVB-vzmIE9AkyIRbzEHBchdKvFfJksmOExZw_anRg4TLJup?purpose=fullsize)

---

## 🔹 Advantage

* Smaller model with good performance

---

## 🔹 Popular Efficient Models

* DistilBERT
* MobileBERT
* TinyBERT

---

## 🔹 Comparison of Techniques

| Technique    | Goal                | Benefit              | Trade-off            |
| ------------ | ------------------- | -------------------- | -------------------- |
| Pruning      | Remove weights      | Smaller model        | Slight accuracy loss |
| Quantization | Reduce precision    | Faster, less memory  | Accuracy drop        |
| Distillation | Train smaller model | Efficient + accurate | Needs teacher model  |
